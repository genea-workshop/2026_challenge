Challenge overview

The challenge is intended for researchers working on speech-driven gesture generation and related non-verbal behaviour modelling. Its goal is to make comparisons between approaches more meaningful by centering the task around shared materials, a common schedule, and a publication track.

The 2026 timeline includes a public launch, release of test sequences, review of publication-track submissions, and an in-person workshop in Malmo. This page can be extended with additional challenge materials and submission instructions as they become available.

For background on the broader series, see the earlier GENEA Challenge pages linked above and the main GENEA site at genea-workshop.github.io.

Call for participation

We invite participation from academia and industry on methods for speech-driven gesture generation and evaluation. GENEA Challenge 2026 is intended as a shared point of comparison for systems that generate embodied non-verbal behaviour from speech and related inputs.

Participants should track the April 12, 2026 launch date and the June 21, 2026 release of test sequences. The publication-track submission deadline is currently listed as June 28 or July 3, 2026, with the final date still to be confirmed.
